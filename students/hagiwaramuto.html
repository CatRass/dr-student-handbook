Description
Hagiwara is a quiet and aloof person, only ever talking when it is needed. She’s also honest to a fault where she hurts people’s feelings unintended. She doesn’t understand empathy or emotions that well, and is also very insecure about that fact, however, she tries her best to learn about people to develop a better understanding. She’s also blind, but that doesn’t mean she’s harmless. If anything, she’s capable of great violence and will not hesitate to use self defense. She instead uses her other senses and her strong intuition to make up for her blindness, and proves to be very effective in confronting danger. But of course, she’s still blind and therefore cannot see.
